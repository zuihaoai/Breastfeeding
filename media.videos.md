# Breastfeeding 教学视频资源清单

## 使用方式

- 优先使用官方页面，不复制视频本体。
- 章号与 `docs/00-目录总表.md` 保持一致。
- 先补动作类，再补概念类。

## 01 孕期认知储备

- [Preparing for Successful Breastfeeding](https://med.stanford.edu/newborns/professional-education/breastfeeding/preparing-for-successful-breastfeeding.html)
  - 来源：Stanford Medicine / First Droplets
  - 用途：产前建立预期、理解正确开始方式
- [Breastfeeding in the First Hour](https://med.stanford.edu/newborns/professional-education/breastfeeding/breastfeeding-in-the-first-hour.html)
  - 来源：Stanford Medicine / First Droplets
  - 用途：黄金一小时的总览式导入

## 02 初乳与开奶

- [Early Initiation of Breastfeeding](https://med.stanford.edu/newborns/professional-education/breastfeeding/early-initiation-of-breastfeeding.html)
  - 来源：Stanford Medicine / Global Health Media
  - 用途：早接触、早吸吮、出生后第一步
- [A Perfect Latch](https://med.stanford.edu/newborns/professional-education/breastfeeding/a-perfect-latch.html)
  - 来源：Stanford Medicine / First Droplets
  - 用途：含乳和起始动作示范

## 03 产后一周

- [How You Know If You Have Enough Milk](https://med.stanford.edu/newborns/professional-education/breastfeeding/abcs-of-breastfeeding/how-you-know-if-you-have-enough-milk.html)
  - 来源：Stanford Medicine
  - 用途：判断摄入是否足够、缓解新手焦虑
- [Common Breastfeeding Mistakes](https://med.stanford.edu/newborns/professional-education/breastfeeding/abcs-of-breastfeeding/common-breastfeeding-mistakes.html)
  - 来源：Stanford Medicine
  - 用途：常见误区拆解
- [If Breastfeeding is Not Successful](https://med.stanford.edu/newborns/professional-education/breastfeeding/abcs-of-breastfeeding/if-breastfeeding-is-not-successful.html)
  - 来源：Stanford Medicine
  - 用途：失败时的下一步与替代方案

## 04 0-6个月纯母乳喂养

- [Hand Expressing Milk](https://med.stanford.edu/newborns/professional-education/breastfeeding/hand-expressing-milk.html)
  - 来源：Stanford Medicine / First Droplets
  - 用途：涨奶、分离、应急挤奶
- [Maximizing Milk Production with Hands-On Pumping](https://med.stanford.edu/newborns/professional-education/breastfeeding/maximizing-milk-production.html)
  - 来源：Stanford Medicine / First Droplets
  - 用途：背奶、泵奶、维持泌乳
- [Storage and Shipping Instructions](https://med.stanford.edu/newborns/professional-education/breastfeeding/storage-and-shipping.html)
  - 来源：Stanford Medicine
  - 用途：乳汁储存与运输规范
